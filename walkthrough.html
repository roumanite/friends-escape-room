Walkthrough

Rachel's beach item

  1. Go to Monica's room and collect rubber bands from the green dresser and chopsticks from the brown dresser.
  2. Combine the chopsticks and rubber band into a giant poking device.
  3. Use the giant poking device to pick Rachel's hat in the kitchen, lodged between the fridge and kitchen counter.

Phoebe's beach item

  1. Collect baby doll wearing white in living room, behind the flower vase.
  2. Collect baby doll wearing brown besides the apartment door.
  3. Collect baby doll wearing black in the guest room's white dresser, on the 3rd shelf.
  4. Go to the bathroom and put the baby doll wearing white on top left corner of the Glynnis' painting.
  5. Put the baby doll wearing black on top right corner.
  6. Put the baby doll wearing brown under the baby doll wearing black.
  7. Pick up Phoebe's guitar that appears under the painting.

Ross' beach item

  1. Pick up Chandler Figurine in the living room, under the TV.
  2. Place the Chandler Figurine in the big box in Monica's bedroom.
  3. Pick up the Midori that appears after the figurine is placed.

Chandler's beach item

Pick up Chandler's glasses on the clothes hanger near the apartment door.

Joey's beach item

  1. Go to Monica's bedroom and collect the coaster from behind the pillows.
  2. In the guest room:
    1. Pick up the onion inside the 1st shelf of the white dresser.
    2. Pick up the cooking oil inside the 2nd shelf of the white dresser.
    3. Pick up the breadcrumbs from behind the pillows.
    4. Collect The Shining from under the blanket.
    5. Collect the green ottoman.
  3. In the living room:
    1. Collect marinara sauce from behind the pink pillow.
    2. Place the coaster under the cup on the table. Pick up the egg that appears after the coaster is placed.
    3. Place the ottoman infront of the sofa with pink pillow. Pick up the meatball clamp that appears.
  4. In the kitchen:
    1. Collect bread rolls from the blue rack.
    2. Click the upper part of the fridge which is the freezer. Place The Shining and a plate of minced beef will appear. Pick up the minced beef.
    3. Exit the freezer and enter the bottom half of the fridge. Collect a plate of Mozarella cheese inside.
    4. Collect the frying pan from the hanging rack in the kitchen.
