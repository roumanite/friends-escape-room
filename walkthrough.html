Walkthrough

Escape Room
  1. Go to the bathroom and pick up the purple box placed inside the bathtub.
  2. Go to the living room and lift the yellow pillow. Pick up the yellow notepad.
  3. Click the laptop. The yellow notepad hints that the laptop password is a translation of "Thy turkey's done". Type in "BING".
  4. A hint for the purple box's code will be displayed. The code is:
    1. Monica's apartment number in that episode - 20
    2. How many claps are there in the theme song? - 4
    3. Which day is Thursday? - 3
    4. Chandler's apartment number in that episode - 19
    5. How many steaks are there? - 5
    6. How many eggplants are there? - 1
    7. How many pages did Ross have to read? - 18
    The code is 2043195118.
  5. Pick up the jellyfish protection lotion once the box is opened.

  2. Combine the chopsticks and rubber band into a giant poking device.
  3. Use the giant poking device to pick Rachel's hat in the kitchen, lodged between the fridge and kitchen counter.

  1. Pick up Chandler's figurine in the living room under the TV.

  1. Go to Monica's bedroom and collect the coaster from behind the pillows.
  2. In the guest room:
    1. Pick up the onion inside the 1st shelf of the white dresser.
    2. Pick up the cooking oil inside the 2nd shelf of the white dresser.
    3. Pick up the breadcrumbs from behind the pillows.
    4. Collect The Shining from under the blanket.
    5. Collect the green ottoman.
  3. In the living room:
    1. Collect marinara sauce from behind the pink pillow.
    2. Place the coaster under the cup on the table. Pick up the egg that appears after the coaster is placed.
    3. Place the ottoman infront of the sofa with pink pillow. Pick up the meatball clamp that appears.
  4. In the kitchen:
    1. Collect bread rolls from the blue rack.
    2. Collect spices from kitchen counter.
    3. Click the upper part of the fridge which is the freezer. Place The Shining and a plate of minced beef will appear. Pick up the minced beef.
    4. Exit the freezer and enter the bottom half of the fridge. Collect a plate of Mozarella cheese inside.
    5. Collect the frying pan from the hanging rack in the kitchen.
  5. Combine minced beef with spices, egg, breadcrumbs, and chopped onions.
  6. Shape the mixture using meatball clamp.
  7. On the stove in the kitchen:
    1. Put the frying pan on the stove.
    2. Pour the cooking oil onto the frying pan.
    3. Turn on the stove and fire will appear.
    4. Put the raw meatball dough on the oil.
    5. Wait a few seconds until the meatballs are fried and the fire disappears by itself.
    6. Pick up the fried meatballs.
  8. Combine the fried meatballs with the bread rolls.
  9. Combine the fried meatballs on bread rolls with marinara sauce.
  10. Combine the fried meatballs on bread rolls with marinara sauce with mozarella cheese.
  11. On the oven in the kitchen:
    1. Put the bread rolls on the oven and close the oven door.
    2. Turn on the oven by clicking the upper part of the oven. The door's color will turn red.
    3. Wait a few seconds until the door is opened. Pick up the ready-to-eat baked meatball sub.
  1. Pick up the handyman tool in the kitchen's blue rack.
  2. Go to the bathroom and use the handyman tool on the tile of the 3rd row and 3rd column from rightmost.
  3. The tile will be pried open and a key will be revealed. Pick up the key.
  4. Once all the beach items are collected, you will be able to use the key to escape Monica's apartment.
